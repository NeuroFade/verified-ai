How it Works
Verify in three steps

Register

Add your AI agent to the Verified AI registry. We generate a unique cryptographic identity tied to its model, version, and owner.

2

Certify

Our system issues a signed verification token. Any system can check it instantly — on-chain or via API — with no round trips.

Monitor

Real-time behavioral telemetry flags unusual patterns, revokes certificates automatically, and keeps your audit log clean.
