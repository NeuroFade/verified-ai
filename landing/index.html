Smart Contracts

Two contracts, one system

Identity layer meets proof layer. Composable by design.

AttestationRegistry

On-chain identity and capability registry for AI inference providers. Stores model metadata, TEE types, endpoint URLs, and reputation scores.

ZKVerifier

zkML proof verification. Stores proof commitments and verifies model outputs on-chain — without re-running inference. Lagrange DeepProve compatible.
